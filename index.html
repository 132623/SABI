Blog & News

Feb 12 - Heartfelt Thanks to Crystal Paints for Transforming Father's Shelter

We extend our deepest gratitude to Crystal Paints and all supporters who generously contributed to the renovation of Father's Shelter children's home. Your kindness has made a lasting impact, enhancing the lives of the children and brightening their future in countless ways.

Your commitment to positive change is truly inspiring. Thanks to your support, the children's home has become a safe, nurturing space where young lives can grow and thrive. We are incredibly grateful for your role in this meaningful transformation.

Thank you, and may your generosity continue to inspire.

Watenda Wema Event at Haven's Children's Home, Nakuru

In conjunction with the Watenda Wema initiative, SABI held a charity drive at Haven's Children's Home in Nakuru. The event was filled with compassion and generosity, as our teams came together to support the children and provide them with much-needed resources. We are grateful for every contribution that helps make a difference in the lives of these young ones.

KGS and SABI Team Up for the Narok Pad Drive

In a powerful collaboration, SABI and KGS launched the Narok Pad Drive, dedicated to providing sanitary products for girls. This drive underscores our shared belief that charity knows no bounds and that every girl deserves the opportunity to stay in school and pursue her dreams.
